ABOUT US

Aquablu Lanka Pvt. Ltd. is Sri Lanka’s most innovative and leading waste management solutions provider led by a team of dedicated domain experts committed to eliminating potentially harmful and environmentally disruptive organic waste. We are a Central Environment Authority (CEA) registered company, deliver world-class solutions for eco-friendly waste management. Our projects have helped dramatically curb waste management inefficiencies in diverse sectors such as Tourism, Housing, Food Processing, Farming, Healthcare and Fisheries.

Our solutions include sludge-less sewage disposal, Industrial waste-water filtration, chemical disposal and solid waste disposal. The latest sustainable, eco-friendly biological and chemical technologies imported from industry leaders underpin our solutions. We partner with Germany’s Aquato and ATB for Sewage Treatment Plants, with Kerasiev for ceramic filtration membranes, h2o.TITANIUM from Spain for chemical free water purification systems and with UK’s Inciner8 for thermal destruction & disposal solutions.

ATB - PUROO® : small treatment plants for households

SBR Process

PUROO® plant works according to the activated sludge principle using the buffering process (SBR plant); the pollutants from the wastewater are absorbed by floating microorganisms (activated sludge) and converted into biomass.

ATB-PUROO® - upto 2000 L/day

A household set-up

Treatment Tanks - we always recommend the tanks to be built underground for gravity feed. Shape or the location of the tanks isn't really matters, but should be water-tight.

Connectivity - toilet and bathing water can be directly connect, where as the kitchen wastewater should always through an oil and grease interceptor to the treatment tank.

Discharge - treated water can be discharge to a flowing water body, re-use for gardening or irrigation purposes, or with a further disinfection for car washing and/or toilet- flushing.    

Operation & Maintenance - plant is driven by a PLC with minimal power. No labour neither maintenance cost is required/involved in the process.

OTHER PRODUCTS & SOLUTIONS

  

DISINFECTION

A chemical-free water purification and disinfection solution which eliminates microorganisms, reduction of turbidity and ordor without generating waste or by-products in the wastewater.

PHYSIO CHEMICAL SOLUTIONS

Physio-chemical solutions applying Ionic coagulation & flocculation principals for the treatment of industrial effluents which contain higher loads of none bio-degradable pollutants and when biological treatments are not feasible.

INCINERATORS

For non-biodegradable and potentially hazardous solid wastes, in the form of robust, high-temperature incinerators which produce low particulate emissions.

MICRO FILTRATION

4th generation ceramic membrane micro filtration equipped with cross-flow filtration and forward-wash advantage for non-biological waste water treatment, which are durable, faster and efficient than polymer membrane or conventional filtration process.
