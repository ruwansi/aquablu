ABOUT US

Aquablu Lanka (Pvt) Ltd rapidly gaining reputation as Sri Lanka’s leading waste management solutions provider led by a team of dedicated domain experts committed to eliminating potentially harmful and environmentally disruptive organic waste.

We deliver world-class solutions for eco-friendly waste management. Our projects have helped dramatically curb waste management inefficiencies across Sri Lanka, in diverse sectors such as Tourism, Housing, Food Processing, Farming, Healthcare and Fisheries.

Our solutions include sludgeless sewage disposal, Industrial waste-water filtration, chemical disposal and solid waste disposal. The latest sustainable, eco-friendly biological and chemical technologies imported from industry leaders underpin our solutions. We partner with Germany’s Aquato and ATB for Sewage Treatment Plants, with Kerasiev for ceramic filtration membranes, and with UK’s Inciner8 for thermal destruction & disposal solutions.

  • WHY CHOOSE US?

CEA Registered company

We are a registered company with the Central Environmental Authority of Sri Lanka as a consulting firm in waste management solutions.

Certified Professionals

Our board of directors consists of experienced professionals qualified in microbiology, chemistry and IT, who personally quality-check the design of our solutions.

Environmental Friendly Cleaning

Our sewage management solutions are fully biological, and are predicated on the latest bio-remediation and bio-augmentation techniques, which are odorless, space saving, and render no sludge.

Best Brands and Systems

Our solutions are facilitated by hardware that has been imported from our highly reputed German and British technology partners.
