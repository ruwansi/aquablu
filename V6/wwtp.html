THE PROCESS
_____

Sophisticated SSB© technology (sequential stabilizing activated sludge) uses in our process, which cyclic the wastewater treatment by sequencing batch process as well as the separation of sludge.

The 3 phases of the SSB® process can be operated in modern single or multi chamber tanks or easily, without major structural changes in existing older tanks. All steps in the plant are carried out on a regular cycle, which is set and driven by the control panel.

A RESIDENTIAL
SET-UP
_____

Treatment Tanks - we always recommend the tanks to be built underground for gravity feed. Shape or the location isn't really matters, but should be water-tight.

Connectivity - toilet and bathing directly and kitchen wastewater always through an oil and grease interceptor.

Discharge - direct to a flowing water body or re-use for gardening, irrigation purposes.
