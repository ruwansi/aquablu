ADVANCED OXIDATION PROCESS (AOP)

_________

The most effective world’s water treatment technology developed by h2o.TITANIUM.

A chemical-free water purification and disinfection solution which eliminates microorganisms, reduction of turbidity and odor without generating waste or by-products in the wastewater. 

h2o.Titanium reactors manufactured from a single block of titanium dioxide (TiO2) whose function is to generate hydroxyl radicals (.OH) to eliminate organic material when activated as a catalyzer by means of UV radiation. The mineralization of organic material in the water gives an indirect boost to its disinfection.
