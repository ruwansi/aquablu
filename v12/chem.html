PHYSIO-CHEMICAL SOLUTIONS

We offer physio-chemical solutions applying Ionic coagulation & flocculation principals for the treatment of industrial effluents which contain higher loads of non bio-degradable pollutants and when biological treatments are not feasible. Batch and Continuous treatment mechanisms are designed to meet the regulatory discharged standards of authorities, and client preferences.
