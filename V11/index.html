THE PROCESS

The patent technology SSB®, cyclic the wastewater by sequencing batch process as well as the separation of sludge. This 3-phase process can be operated in single or multi chamber tanks, or easily without major structural changes in existing older tanks. All steps in the process are carried out on a regular cycle, which is set and driven by the PLC unit.
