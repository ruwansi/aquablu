ADVANCED OXIDATION PROCESS (AOP)
"THE MOST & EFFECTIVE WORLD'S WATER TREATMENT TECHNOLOGY"
_____ 

The h2o.TITANIUM has been specially designed to disinfect any kind of water (even extremely level of infected water), achieving the 99.99% of microorganisms inactivation, without using any chemical product and, therefore, removing the biocide or UV radiation often existing. Due the oxidation power of the hydroxyl radicals, is able to mineralize any kind of molecule contained on it C‐C structure links until to transform it in CO2 and H2O.

Low energy consumption : 15-20 W / m3 treated water.
Simplicity : No need for infrastructure, Its installation is carried out in by-pass.
Minimum Cost : Energy, operation and maintenance.
Compact System : Minimum area of ​​occupation.
ECO Technology : Does not use chemicals.
Durable : Useful life over 25 years.
Zero Maintenance : No human interaction, fully automated
