Incineration Solutions 

Hazardous and problematic waste can be completely destroyed via incineration, leaving only harmless ash to be disposed of safely.
We offer a range of Incinerators for all applications in the waste management industry, our products are specifically designed with clean air incineration at the forefront of our product development for Medical, Animal by product and General Municipal waste streams.
All of our products have been rigorously tested to ensure the suitability within their respective waste sectors. We partner with “Inciner8”, a globally respected manufacturing organization in UK, for the systems for all applications in the solid waste management industry.


Medical Waste

Medical range is specifically designed for medical/pharmaceutical, hazardous and hospital waste management. Capable of disposing of type IV pathological waste and all medical waste.

General & Municipal Waste

General waste is an unwanted refuse material or substance. It may consist of materials from community or household activities (municipal, domestic or camp waste) or from industrial activities (manufacturing, mining or agricultural).

Animal/Poultry Waste

Animal diseases, viruses and the spread of infection are a few of the dangers of poor animal waste treatment. Disposal through incineration is the best way to ensure bio-security.
