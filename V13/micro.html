The Technology

Ceramic membrane are prepared by extrusion technique followed by sintering (firing) at high temperature (1000oC to 1500oC). It requires high energy to form (bond), following which below are the advantages of ceramic membrane over polymer membrane:

"Need Innovation (NI)", our partner developed a proprietary sustainable technology for manufacturing ultra filtration (UF) Inorganic (ceramic) membrane and is engaged in manufacturing this UF inorganic membrane and the module/filter equipment/unit for filtration solution under the trade mark of ‘KerasievTM’ in class 9 and 11 for the first time in India. The KerasievTM UF inorganic membrane are capable for handling corrosive and non-corrosive liquids between pH 2 and 13. The membrane has huge life of 15 years compared to 6 -12 months of polymer membrane.
