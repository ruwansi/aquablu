Company Profile

Aquablu Lanka Pvt. Ltd. is a Sri Lanka’s leading waste management solutions provider led by a team of dedicated domain experts committed to eliminating potentially harmful and environmentally disruptive organic waste. We are a Central Environment Authority (CEA) registered company, deliver world-class solutions for eco-friendly waste management.
Our solutions include sludge less sewage disposal, Industrial waste-water filtration, Physio-chemical solutions, chemical free water disinfection and purification, solid waste disposal and Air purification and disinfection.
Our technologies are the latest and most effective in their industry, used internationally to obtain high discharge values of effluents. The flexible solutions can be customized to fit in to limited spaces, minimum monitoring and maintenance, economical and ease of assembly. The solutions are well proven for Hotels, Condominium apartments, Factories, Food processing factories, Restaurants, Schools or any residential setups, which has limited space and in the need of high standards of discharged water.
We partner with Germany’s Aquato® and ATB® for Sewage Treatment Plants, Kerasiev® for ceramic filtration membranes, h2o.TITANIUM from Spain for chemical free water disinfection solutions, Wizard Technologies from Spain for Air disinfection solutions and with UK’s Inciner8 for thermal destruction & disposal solutions.

Dr. Ananda Thennakoon
Chairman

Dr. Ananda holds more than three decades of industry experience as a research scientist, presently the deputy director at Coconut Research Institute, Sri Lanka. He was the Principal Soil Scientist and Head of the Soils & Plant Nutrition Division. His major contributions were, involved in research & technology transfer activities, carry out major studies, advisories, surveys, service functions, contribution to symposia, recommendations to the national development, academic involvements & supervision, media contributions, organizing & conducting workshops for community groups etc,. Dr. Ananda is member of British Society of Soil Science, Sri Lanka Association for the Advancement of Science., Sri Lanka Institute of Biology and Sri Lanka Society of Soil Science. He holds PhD (Soil Chemistry & Microbiology) from University of Aberdeen UK, M Phil (botany) from University of Kelaniya and BSc (Agri) from University of Ruhuna.

Ushantha Ekanayake
Managing Director

Ushantha oversees the company direction, provision technical consultancy, international trade and business development activities. He’s the founding director of the company with vast experience in the fields of microbiology, wastewater treatment and fiber technology. Ushantha was the former CEO of Klaro, a wastewater treatment company and Managing director of AJ Fishing, a producer of high quality commercial fishing equipment and fiber solutions for Norwegian countries. Ushantha is a graduate in Microbiology from the University of Kelaniya.

Ruwan Silva
Operations Director

Ruwan leads a cross-functional team responsible for research, implementation and IT. He oversees the design and development of automated water treatment process flows, and drives the continuous improvement of the company’s IT infrastructure. He has two decades of experience in IT Services, having worked for the technology consulting divisions of several global firms. Ruwan was Manager, Engineering at Pearson for 9 years, leading specialized shared services such as Release Engineering, Environment Management and Application Support. He was previously a Technology Consultant at Virtusa Corp. for 8 years. Ruwan is a graduate in Mathematics and Statistics from the University of Kelaniya.
