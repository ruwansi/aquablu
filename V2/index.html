ABOUT US

Aquablu Lanka Pvt. Ltd. rapidly gaining reputation as Sri Lanka’s leading waste management solutions provider led by a team of dedicated domain experts committed to eliminating potentially harmful and environmentally disruptive organic waste. We are a Central Environment Authority (CEA) registered company, deliver world-class solutions for eco-friendly waste management. Our projects have helped dramatically curb waste management inefficiencies in diverse sectors such as Tourism, Housing, Food Processing, Farming, Healthcare and Fisheries.

Our solutions include sludgeless sewage disposal, Industrial waste-water filtration, chemical disposal and solid waste disposal. The latest sustainable, eco-friendly biological and chemical technologies imported from industry leaders underpin our solutions. We partner with Germany’s Aquato and ATB for Sewage Treatment Plants, with Kerasiev for ceramic filtration membranes, h2o.TITANIUM from Spain for chemical free water purification plants and with UK’s Inciner8 for thermal destruction & disposal solutions. 

SEWAGE TREATMENT PLANTS

Our advanced sewage treatment plants are fully bio-logical, powered by the latest SSB technology, which is the advanced version of SBR technology, designed and fabricated in Germany by AQUATO and ATB who are industry leaders and our long time partners.

Eco-friendly Cleaning
fully bio-logical, no chemical involvement in the process

Odorless
system is fully aerobic, thus no bad gases will be generated

 Sludge-less
minimum sludge removals just for non bio-degradable particles

Safe & Easy 
No moving/mechanical parts, electricity or submersible pumps underneath the water

PLC controlled
Highly controllable and re-programmable to suite any requirement & helps saving power consumption

Small Foot-print
very compact system, comparatively 50% reduction of the size to a conventional system

ATB - PUROO® : small treatment plants for households

SBR® Process

PUROO® Complete plant also works according to the activated sludge principle using the buffering process (SBR plant); the pollutants from the wastewater are absorbed by floating microorganisms (activated sludge) and converted into biomass.

ATB-PUROO® - upto 2000 L/day

A typical household setup

Treatment Tanks - we recommend the tanks to be built underground for gravity feed. Shape or the location isn't matter as long as it can handle the incoming water volume and should be water-tight.

Connectivity - toilet and bathing water can directly connect where kitchen waste water thru an oil and grease interceptor to the treatment tank.

Discharge - treated water can be directly use for gardening or irrigation purposes. Or with further disinfection, useful car washing and toilet- flushing.

Operation & Maintenance - plant is driven by a PLC with minimal power. No labour neither maintenance cost is required/involved.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Dr. Ananda Thennakoon (Chairman)

Dr. Ananda holds more than three decades of industry experience as a research scientist, presently the deputy director at Coconut Research Institute, Sri Lanka. He was the Principal Soil Scientist and Head of the Soils & Plant Nutrition Division. His major contributions were, involved in research & technology transfer activities, carry out major studies, advisories, surveys, service functions, contribution to symposia, recommendations to the national development, academic involvements & supervision, media contributions, organizing & conducting workshops for community groups etc,.
Dr. Ananda is member of British Society of Soil Science, Sri Lanka Association for the Advancement of Science., Sri Lanka Institute of Biology and Sri Lanka Society of Soil Science. He holds PhD (Soil Chemistry & Microbiology) from University of Aberdeen UK, M Phil (botany) from University of Kelaniya and BSc (Agri) from University of Ruhuna. 

Ushantha Ekanayake (Managing Director)

Ushantha oversees the company direction, provision technical consultancy, international trade and business development activities. He’s the founding director of the company with vast experience in the fields of microbiology, wastewater treatment and fiber technology.
Ushantha was the former CEO of Klaro, a wastewater treatment company and Managing director of AJ Fishing, a producer of high quality commercial fishing equipment and fiber solutions for Norwegian countries.
Ushantha is a graduate in Microbiology from the University of Kelaniya. 

Ruwan Silva (Director Operations)

Ruwan leads a cross-functional team responsible for research, implementation and IT. He oversees the design and development of automated water treatment process flows, and drives the continuous improvement of the company’s IT infrastructure.
He has two decades of experience in IT Services, having worked for the technology consulting divisions of several global firms. Ruwan was Manager, Engineering at Pearson for 9 years, leading specialized shared services such as Release Engineering, Environment Management and Application Support. He was previously a Technology Consultant at Virtusa Corp. for 8 years.
Ruwan is a graduate in Mathematics and Statistics from the University of Kelaniya.
